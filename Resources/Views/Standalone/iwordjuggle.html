iWordJuggle

Simple, yet challenging word game. The game that leaves out "features" to stand out of the crowd. As the saying goes, less is more.

Sold.

PICKING PHASE

You are given a set of 15 words from which you have to pick 3 to be your game words. You can random the words again and again, but be wise, you have to pick all your words from the same set!

LANDSCAPE

It also supports left handed mode, displaying the words and check button on the left and the points on the right.

SIMPLE GAMEPLAY

Create words without having a timer nor a find this many words counter to bug you. Play at your own pace, put it down, come back later. As simple as that

SAVE

Simple and elegant main menu. As any game should, it saves the current state of your game when you exit, so you can come back later.
