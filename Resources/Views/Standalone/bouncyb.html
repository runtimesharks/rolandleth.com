Bouncy B

This is what I like to call an inverted endless runner. Instead of running and jumping from platform to platform, here you are constantly falling and need to bounce on the platforms, or fall to your shameless defeat.

Sold.

SMASH MOVE

Furiously dive downwards, smashing the cloud you land on.

B gets angry for 1.5 seconds.

LIFT MOVE

Cautiously lift yourself when in danger of falling.

B gets sick for 6 seconds.

STARS

What kind of runner game would this be without collectibles? Gather as many stars as you can!

FLIGHT

Burst forward in a dashing flight, at dazzling speeds!

SIMPLE TO USE CONTROLS

Touch the left side to give B a lift from below. Touch the right side to instantly send B downwards. Controls are inverted if left handed mode is active.
